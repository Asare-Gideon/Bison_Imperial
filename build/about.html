About Us

Bison Imperial Global Shipping and Logistics is a fast-expanding shipping and logistics service provider headquartered in the biggest port enclave of Ghana (Tema) with presence in Accra and Kumasi.

  • Evolution Of Shipping Solutions

    Our shipping services evolve to meet modern business demands, adapting to market changes and customer expectations for optimal performance and reliability.

  • Integrity In Every Action

    We operate with the highest ethical standards, ensuring that every decision is made with transparency and accountability, building lasting trust with our clients and partners.

  • The Power Of Teamwork

    Collaboration is at the heart of our success, where every team member, partner, and client works together toward shared goals, driving efficiency and innovation in our operations.

Our Mission

To ensure that our clients and other stakeholders enjoy the best service and are competitive by not only delivering ahead of schedule, but also in an innovative, efficient and effective way.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be the most sought-after shipping and logistics provider in Africa, known for our excellence, reliability, and innovative solutions. We strive to set new industry standards by delivering efficient and customer-focused services across the continent
