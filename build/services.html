Customs Brokerage & Clearance

Bison Global Shipping and Logistics is inundated with staff and management with excellent understanding of trade prerequisite, procedures, customs tariff and valuation regulations. Bison Global Shipping and Logistics is committed to ensuring that our clients enjoy the fastest customs clearance of their cargo in order to avoid demurrage and/or storage payment.

  • Staff and management with expert knowledge of trade procedures and customs regulations
  • Comprehensive understanding of customs tariffs and valuation
  • Commitment to fast customs clearance for all cargo

Warehousing & Ex-warehousing

Bison Global Shipping and Logistics through its warehouse partners situated at strate- gic locations in the country (ie Tema, Accra, Kumasi, Takoradi, Sunyani and Tamale) gives our clients the advantage of keeping their raw material and/or finished products closer to where they are most needed.

Bison Global Shipping and Logistics leverages these strategically located warehouses to offer flexible storage solutions, ensuring efficient distribution and reduced transit times. This proximity allows our clients to streamline their supply chains, minimize costs, and maintain quicker access to essential materials or products, enhancing overall operational efficiency.

Haulage

Ghana’s two sea ports (ie Tema and Takora- di) have been one of the main ports serving it’s landlocked west African neighbors. Bison Global Shipping and Logistics provides very efficient and cost-effective haulage service for both home consumption and transit cargo. With a site already acquired at the Boankra Inland Port in Kumasi, we seek to continue to provide the best transport services for our clients

  • Efficient, cost-effective haulage services for local and transit cargo
  • Serving both Tema and Takoradi ports for West African neighbors
  • Strategic site at Boankra Inland Port in Kumasi.
